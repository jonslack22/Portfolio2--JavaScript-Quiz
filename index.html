Instructions:

A series of 11 multiple choice questions will be displayed, one after another, each with three different clickable answers to choose from. There is no time limit for these questions.

Clicking an answer will generate a message informing you if you got it right or wrong, as well as a corresponding sound effect from Waluigi himself. From here, clicking 'OK' will generate the next question.

After all questions have been answered, the quiz will end, and your final score will be displayed
